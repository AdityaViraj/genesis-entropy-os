Registers are just varibales : eax , ebx , ecx edx are like our "fastest" variables. We move data into them to do math or prepare for function call.
Memory is an array: Evrything in the computer is just an address in a gaint list. mov[0xB8000],'A' meand " Go to address 0xB8000 (the screen) and write letter 'A'
Jumps are control flow: There are no if staements or while loops in Assembly. There are only labels ( like start: ) and jumps ( like jmp start). We are literally telling the CPU where to move its next eye.
